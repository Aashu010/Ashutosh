ABOUT_ME

MY JOURNEY IN CYBERSECURITY

My fascination with hacking began in childhood, when movies made hackers look as if they possessed a supernatural power over technology. That sense of mystery sparked my curiosity and soon turned into a dream — one day, I wanted to be a hacker too. What started as imagination gradually evolved into passion, and today, that same energy drives my journey in ethical hacking. Now I use those skills not to break but to protect — designing secure systems and safeguarding the digital world..

I specialize in identifying vulnerabilities before they can be exploited, combining technical knowledge with creative problem-solving to anticipate potential attack vectors. My approach to security is proactive rather than reactive - I believe in building secure systems from the ground up rather than just patching vulnerabilities.

When I'm not hunting for bugs or studying new attack techniques, I contribute to open-source security tools and participate in CTF challenges to continuously sharpen my skills. I'm driven by the belief that in our increasingly digital world, cybersecurity is not just a technical challenge but a fundamental requirement for trust and safety online.
